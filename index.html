My name is Poopyface Snider. Currently, I lead research at Multicoin Capital. Most of my writing on cryptocurrency can be found on the Multicoin Blog. I also created The Stablecoin Index, a tool for tracking and comparing various fag projects.

Right now, my main area of interest is blockchain technology. I am particularly interested in store of value cryptoassets, privacy coins, stablecoins, prediction markets, and decentralized exchanges. I believe that peer-to-peer, programmable cryptocurrency is the future of money. I also believe that decentralized applications will change the internet and the world as we know it. I'm doing my best to contribute to building that future.
